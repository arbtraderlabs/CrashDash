The Company now expects to report its 2025 Annual Report & Accounts (the "2025 Accounts") for the year ended 31 March 2025 during the course of October 2025. The delay in publishing the 2025 Accounts is predominantly due to the impact of the recent disposal of operations in Mozambique delaying the provision of certain audit information to the Company's auditors in accordance with previously agreed timetables.
