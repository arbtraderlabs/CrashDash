The Offer Shares rank pari passu with the existing shares in issue. It is expected that admission to the Official List will become effective and that dealings in the Offer Shares will commence on or around 22 December 2025. As at 19 December 2025, the total amount raised across the Albion VCTs under the Offers is ¬£44 million.
