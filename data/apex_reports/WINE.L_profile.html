All Ordinary Shares purchased under the RABB Buyback shall be classified as shares held in treasury. Such treasury shares have no voting rights at the Company's general meetings. Following the settlement of the RABB Buyback on 29 December 2025, the Company's issued ordinary share capital will be 68,950,415 Ordinary Shares, with 2,766,665 held in treasury and the total number of voting rights in the Company will be 68,950,415.
