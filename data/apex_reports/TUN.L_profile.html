Introduction The Company announced on 6 February 2026 that it had conditionally raised, in aggregate, ¬£43.04 million (before expenses) through the proposed issue of the Fundraising Shares pursuant to the Fundraising, comprising the Subscription, Placing and the Retail Offer. The Fundraising is being undertaken in two tranches as the Company does not have sufficient shareholder authorities to issue all of the Fundraising Shares. The Company is seeking additional shareholder authorities to, inter alia , issue the Second Tranche Subscription Shares, Second Tranche Placing Shares and the Retail Offer Shares.
