If approved, this formulation may also offer an alternative approach for adults who can't swallow our current capsule formulation. Anders Lundstrom, CEO of Shield, commented: "We are delighted with the FDA approval of ACCRUFeR¬Æ, making it available to the adolescent population, who now can benefit from a much needed safe and effective oral iron treatment."
