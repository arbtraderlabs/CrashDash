Data from healthy participants support the proposed mechanism of action of VLP Peanut. In addition, preliminary blinded biomarker data collected three months after last dosing of peanut allergic patients indicate a sustained biomarker response . The Company intends to carry out a second interim analysis in peanut-allergic patients receiving the highest doses in due course.
