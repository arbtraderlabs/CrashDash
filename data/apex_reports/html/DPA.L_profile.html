8 Feb 2023 14:00 RNS Number : 3464P DP Aircraft I Limited 08 February 2023 Price Monitoring Extension The auction call period has been extended in this security by 5 minutes. Auction call extensions give London Stock Exchange electronic order book users a further opportunity to review the prices and sizes of orders entered in an individual security's auction call before the execution occurs.
