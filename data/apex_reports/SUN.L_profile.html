The Company announces the appointments of Roy Davis, Andrew Boteler and Duncan Soukup as Non-Executive Chair, Independent Non-Executive Director and Non-Executive Director respectively, with immediate effect. Roy Davis Roy is an experienced executive with over 35 years of global experience in a variety of industries including medtech, diagnostics and technology sectors. He is currently Chairman of Inspiration Healthcare Group plc, a leading neonatal respiratory company, and Foster & Freeman Ltd (the trading name of the Galton group of companies) , a leading forensic imaging manufacturer.
