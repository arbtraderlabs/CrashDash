Successful completion of Definitive Feasibility Study On 23 December 2025, the Company announced the successful completion of the DFS for the Cinovec Project. The Study confirms Cinovec as a long-life producer of battery grade lithium carbonate with a forecast production of 37,500 tpa. This production rate equates to approximately 5.2% of forecast EU demand in 2030 and sufficient for the production of approximately 1.3 million 60kWh batteries per annum. These numbers underscore the importance of the Cinovec Project in the supply chain for Electric Vehicles in the EU.
