Consumer confidence remains low across the UK and many European markets which in some cases has created a highly price competitive situation for certain products. As the largest UK-based retailer in our sector, our focus is maintaining a disciplined approach to product pricing, whilst reconfiguring the business to ensure the Group is profitable irrespective of market conditions.
