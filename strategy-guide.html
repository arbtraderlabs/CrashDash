5 Key Metrics to Study

Multi-factor scoring system that helps you learn about:

  • Momentum shifts (trend exhaustion?)

Drawdown Percentage

  • Understand which patterns tend to work vs. fail
  • Learn from both winners and losers

Best Historical Rally

Peak gain achieved from similar past signals on this ticker. Study this to:

  • Understand upside potential
  • See if ticker has history of violent recoveries
  • Compare current setup to historical patterns
