The educational crash-recovery research tool for people with real lives

What Is CrashDash?

CrashDash is an educational stock research scanner built for people who don't want to manually sift through hundreds of charts every day.

It helps you learn about crashed, unloved, and forgotten stocks that might be setting up for recovery‚Äîthe kind of price movements most people only notice after they've already happened.

The concept is simple:

  • Check it once a day
  • Review the most interesting educational setups in a few minutes
  • Get back to your life, job, family, or whatever you actually care about

No trading room.
No 10 screens.
No fake Instagram lifestyle.

Just educational research signals you can study to improve your understanding of market behavior.

Who Built This (And Why)

CrashDash was built by someone who:

  • Used to scan stocks manually after work
  • Found it impossible to keep up while working full-time
  • Hated the idea of day trading all day for tiny gains
  • Wanted to focus on bigger educational patterns over days, weeks, or even years

The idea was simple:

"If the best educational opportunities are the quiet ones nobody talks about, there must be a way to scan for them automatically."

So over several years, the rules, patterns, and ideas from manual scanning were turned into an engine‚Äîand that engine became CrashDash.

This is a research and education tool, not a "get rich quick" scheme. It's designed to help you learn about market patterns, not to tell you what to trade.

Why Not Day Trading?

Let's be honest:

  • Real day trading is a grind, not a lifestyle flex
  • Professionals sit through stress, noise, and micro-moves all day
  • Most Instagram "traders" sell a lifestyle, not results

CrashDash is built for people who:

  • Don't want to trade 20 times a day
  • Don't want to quit their job to sit in front of charts
  • Prefer one quick educational scan, a few good research ideas, then back to normal life

We focus on learning about crashes, consolidations, and recovery potential‚Äînot scalping pennies all day.
